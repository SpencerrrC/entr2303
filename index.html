Food Packages

ELEVENO offers four food packages:

  • BASELINE ($30/pp)
  • MID-COURT ($40/pp)
  • THE ELEVENO ($50pp)
  • MORNING SERVE ($25/pp)

All include Appetizers, Handhelds, Flatbreads, and Dips, replenished for two hours.

Beverage Packages

Three packages are available:

  • THE COURTSIDE (starting at $25/2hrs)
  • THE MATCH POINT (starting at $35/2hrs) with House Spirits
  • THE CHAMPIONSHIP (starting at $45/2hrs) with Premium Spirits.

Drink tickets or consumption bar also available

Upgrades / Add-Ons

Enhance with food like a Signature Salad Bar, Charcuterie & Cheese Board, and New England Seafood Tower.

Drink Add-Ons include Custom Welcome Cocktails/Mocktails and a Mobile Courtside Bar.

Event Enhancements

Customize your event (Inquire for Pricing). Options include Half Center Court Bar or Full Venue Rental.

Pickleball additions include a Coach/Host, Tournament Support, and Branded Paddles or Custom Trophies.
